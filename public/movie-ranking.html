BEST INDIAN MOVIES TO WATCH THIS SUMMER

TOP 3 Picks

1. Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (DDLJ)

'DDLJ' is An Immortal Film! It's among those "FINE" films, that remain perfect forever. It's among those gems, that can never go dated. Be it the direction, the screenplay, the songs, the performances, the cinematography...everything just gets better!

A Must watch movie for Rom Com Lovers!

2. Kabhi Khushi Khabi Gham (K3G)

All in all, Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham has bits of everything: drama, melodrama, romance and comedy, all presented wholeheartedly in true Hindi-film style with lots of tears and lots of fun. It is an example of Bollywood entertainment at its grandest, and works as such.

This is a movie to be seen with one's family !

3. Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara (ZNMD)

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara' is a Stunning film. Zoya Akhtar delivers a film, that is full of human-emotions & energy. The Writer-Director has handled the film fabulously and she indeed deserves immense praise. Also, the performances by all the actors aboard, are top-notch!

This movie is a must watch movie with your best friends !
